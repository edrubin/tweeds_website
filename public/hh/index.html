Happy hour

Some ideas for happy hour...

Non-alcoholic cucumber collins

  • - 4 oz. soda water
  • - Lime juice (1 lime)
  • - Lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
  • - Agave nectar to taste
  • - 3 slices of cucmber
Add juice and agave to soda water. Stir briefly. Top with cucumber. Garnish: Mint (or more citrus).

Amaro spritz

  • - 3 oz. soda water
  • - 2 oz. amaro (try Cynar and/or )
  • - 2 oz. fresh lemon juice (1/2 a lemon)
  • - 2+ dashes of your favorite bitters (best: black walnut)
Pour all ingredients over a glass nearly filled with ice. Garnish with anything—savory is surprisingly nice (e.g., olives).

Non-alcoholic: Swap out the amaro for cold-brew concentrate (or anything bitter—e.g., tea).

Negroni

  • - 1 oz. gin
  • - 1 oz. Campari
  • - 1 oz. sweet vermouth
Stir with ice. Strain over ice. Garnish: Citrus peel (Note: Teevrat says this citrus peel must be torched first).

New Spain

  • - One 1/2-inch (1cm) thick slice of fresh ginger, peeled
  • - 1/2 oz. agave nectar
  • - 3/4 oz. fresh squeezed lime juice
  • - 2 oz. dry sherry
  • - 1 oz. best-quality mezcal such as Del Maguey Vida
Muddle ginger, agave, and lime juice in a shaker. Add sherry, mezcal, and ice (fill halfway). Shake. Strain onto ice. Garnish: Lime slice and nutmeg.
Source: David Lebovitz

A very good margarita

  • - 1 to 1.5 oz. lime juice (fresh)
  • - 2 oz. water
  • - 1 to 2 oz. tequila (maybe better: mezcal)
  • - 1/2 oz agave nectar (to taste)
Pour ingredients over ice-filled shaker and shake. Strain (over ice, if desired).
Bonus points: Make it spicy (e.g., Scrappy's Firewater Tincture)
Source: Averie Cooks

Old Fashioned

  • - 1–2 teaspoons simple syrup (or 1 sugar cube)
  • - 2–3 dashes bitters (classic: Angostura)
  • - 2 oz. bourbon/rye whiskey
Add simple syrup (or sugar) and bitters to mixing glass and mix. Add ice and whiskey; mix. Garnish: orange peel and/or cherry.

Manhattan

  • - 2 oz. whiskey (esp. rye)
  • - 1 oz. sweet vermouth
  • - 2+ dashes bitters
Pour ingredients over ice. Stir. Strain. Garnish: Cherry.

Rum, lime, cola

  • Juice from 1 lime
  • - 4 oz. cola (i.e., Coke)
  • - 2 oz. rum
Squeeze lime into Collins glass. Add ice, rum, and the "remains" of the lime. Stir.

White Russian

  • - 1 1/2 oz. vodka (or try rum)
  • - 3/4 oz. coffee liquer
  • - 3/4 oz. heavy cream
Pour vodka and coffee liquer over ice. Top with cream. Stir (or shake).
