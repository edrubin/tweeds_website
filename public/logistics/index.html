Traveling

Air travel: Most people will fly into Eugene (EUG). Eugene has a small airport with direct flights to many regional hubs (Seattle, Portland, Denver, SF, LA, etc.). Security lines are typically quite short but flight-time options can be limited.

The airport is a bit outside of town, so you'll need to find a way to get into Eugene once you land. Uber and Lyft both operate in Eugene. You might have to wait a bit if your flight arrives late at night (consider scheduling a pick up with Uber, Lyft, or a taxi service). You can also rent a car at the airport.

Train: Eugene also has an Amtrak stop.

Portland: You could also consider flying into Portland and then two hours to Eugene. Probably not the best option for most people.
